## Vendor Details

### 1. monday.com
- **Direct affiliate URL:** https://monday.com/affiliate-program
- **Commission:** Tier model — base 25% → up to 100% first-year sales as volume grows; monthly payouts via PayPal or Stripe
- **Cookie:** 90 days
- **Traffic/approval requirement:** None disclosed publicly — applies direct from site, no traffic minimum stated
- **Notes:** Very clean direct program, no network middleman. Highest upside commission in this set at volume.
- **Priority:** P0 — apply first

### 2. Brevo (formerly Sendinblue)
- **Direct affiliate URL:** https://www.brevo.com/partners/affiliates/
- **Commission:** $5 per free account + $100 per paying customer
- **Payout:** PayPal or Stripe, alternatives available for non-PayPal regions
- **Traffic/approval requirement:** None disclosed. Standard bloggers/creators program — no stated minimum.
- **Notes:** Brevo affiliate dashboard runs on PartnerStack internally (per their help docs), BUT you apply directly via brevo.com/partners/affiliates — the public-facing entry point is their own brand page. Once approved, they give PartnerStack access. Applying directly to Brevo means PartnerStack approves you *for Brevo specifically* — this is different from the generic PartnerStack marketplace rejection.
- **Priority:** P0 — apply directly to Brevo, not via PartnerStack marketplace

### 3. Kit (ConvertKit)
- **Direct affiliate URL:** https://kit.com/affiliate
- **Commission:** 50% recurring for first 12 months; 10–20% recurring lifetime after year 1 (status-gated)
- **Cookie:** 90 days
- **Traffic/approval requirement:** No paying customer requirement confirmed (per community sources). Open to publishers.
- **Notes:** Strongest recurring commission in this set. Email marketing category maps well to our SaaS audience.
- **Priority:** P0 — apply immediately

### 4. Apollo.io
- **Direct affiliate URL:** https://www.apollo.io/partners/affiliates
- **Commission:** 15% per monthly paid referral, 20% per annual paid referral, for first 12 months. Over 4,000 active partners.
- **Traffic/approval requirement:** Short application form — no traffic minimum stated. 4,000+ partner base suggests low bar.
- **Notes:** Apollo is a B2B sales tool — perfect fit for our SaaS-deal-buying audience. Direct program, no network required.
- **Priority:** P0 — apply immediately

### 5. Gorgias
- **Direct affiliate URL:** https://www.gorgias.com/partner-program (or https://partners.gorgias.com/)
- **Commission:** "Industry-leading revenue share" — % not publicly stated; likely 10–20% based on prior PartnerStack listing
- **Cookie:** Unknown
- **Traffic/approval requirement:** ⚠️ Program appears agency/agency-partner focused ("agencies and call centers"). Not a publisher/blogger affiliate program — may require existing client base.
- **Notes:** Deprioritize. Not a fit for a directory/publisher model. Recheck if Gorgias launches a public publisher affiliate program later.
- **Priority:** ⚠️ LOW — skip for now, revisit after 5k monthly visitors
