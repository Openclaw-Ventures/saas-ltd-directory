SaaS LTD Deals is an independent directory tracking lifetime deals for SaaS software tools. We aggregate deals from AppSumo, DealFuel, DealMirror, Dealify, and other platforms — updated nightly via automated scraping.

## How It Works

Our scrapers check major deal platforms every night at 2am UTC. New deals are added automatically. Expired deals are flagged. Prices are verified on each run.

## Our Editorial Process

Every deal gets an automated verdict based on rating, review count, and savings percentage. Deals with 4.5+ stars and 100+ verified reviews earn a "Strong Buy" recommendation. We flag deals with fewer than 20 reviews as unverified.

## Affiliate Disclosure

We earn a commission when you purchase through our links, at no extra cost to you. This funds the directory and keeps it free. We list deals regardless of whether an affiliate program exists — our goal is comprehensive coverage, not commission maximisation.
